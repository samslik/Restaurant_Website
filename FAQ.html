Here are our Top 5 most frequently asked questions

1. Do you sell gift cards?

YES! These are now available to purchase at all our pizzerias, find out more here

2. Do you do a gluten free pizza?

I’m afraid that we don’t.

3. Do you run pizza making classes?

YES! We hold a master class on the second Monday of every month in Chiswick and the second Tuesday of every month in Brighton Marina. The class lasts approximately 2 ½ hours and costs £20.00 (inc VAT). To find out more or to book, please email chiswick@francomanca.co.uk OR call on 020 8747 4822 and brightonmarina@francomanca.co.uk or call on 01273 977 028

4. Do you do take-away?

There are 3 ways you can do this… Modern (for us)… choose your local & click on the order link, order, pay…go and collect Less modern…phone your local, place the order, go in, pay and take out Really old fashioned…go to your local, place your order, wait and leave as it comes straight out the oven

5. Do you take bookings?

We’ve always run a first come, first serve policy. If on arrival you find there’s a queue, don’t worry as you shouldn’t need to wait too long. We do have some exceptions to our booking policy for large groups at some pizzerias and are now holding back a few tables for reservation. Please visit your local pizzeria page to find out more.
